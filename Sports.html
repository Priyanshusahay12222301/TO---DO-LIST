Benefits of Playing Sport

  • Improves cardiovascular health
  • Enhances muscular strength and endurance
  • Develops agility, coordination, and balance
  • Promotes teamwork and social interaction
  • Boosts mood and reduces stress

Health Benefits of Sport

  • Helps in weight management and reduces the risk of obesity
  • Reduces the risk of chronic diseases such as heart disease, diabetes, and hypertension
  • Improves bone density and reduces the risk of osteoporosis
  • Enhances flexibility and joint mobility
  • Improves mental health and cognitive function
